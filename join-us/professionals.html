PROFESSIONALS
Join the children’s movement to democratize
creativity and spread empathy universally.
We believe that children are creators and builders of the future. Their imagination is social capital and their art opens a window into their world. Children's voices should be heard all around, from boardrooms and town halls to the Congress and the UN. Their art should be in every contemporary art museum and on the walls of each business office. You can help us empower the children by reforming education to foster creativity and infuse it with empathy.
How to contribute
  • You can encourage your school to participate in the 7th Arts Olympiad in 2023.

  • You can host a workshop or volunteer at the 7th World Children's Festival in the summer of 2024 at the National Mall across the U.S. Capitol. The World Children’s Festival

  • You can ask us to arrange a children's panel at your conference or exhibit children's art at your annual meeting.

  • You can adopt the ICAF as your charity by making a tax-deductible donation today.
