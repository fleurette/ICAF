Partnerships

ICAF has program partners or representatives in nearly 100 countries. In the United States, ICAF works directly with schools and after-school programs.

Organizations that have partnered with ICAF include:

  • Collaboration on ChildArt magazine to inspire a new generation of architects.

  • ICAF judged the Endangered Species School Art Contest entries.

  • Keynote address at the World Congress and Award to ICAF

  • The State Education Standard

  • Financial support and collaboration on Art for Health.

  • Partnership to publish and promote initial findings of the seminal ABCD Study.

  • A collaboration to celebrate creative youth and the power of our “imagiNations."

  • World Conference on Arts Education and UNESCO Observatory publication of ICAF research.

  • World Congress and publication of research.

  • World Academy of Art and Science

  • Partnership on a program titled “Destruction, Reconstruction, and Prevention.”

  • Collaboration on Expanding Awareness of Mental Health in Childhood and Adolescence.
