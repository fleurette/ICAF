      I began by consulting with Dr. Stephen Olmore, a geologist, to
      better understand the concept of soil profiles and underground
      geological structures. Based on what I learned from Dr. Ol-
      more, I decided to build three tubes each one representing a
      different soil profile. The purpose was to run water samples
      through each of the three tubes, extract samples and analyze
      what change, if any, could be detected after the water perco-
      lated down the different layers of material.
