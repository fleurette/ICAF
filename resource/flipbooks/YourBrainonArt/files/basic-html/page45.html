                                                                                   RHYTHM
                                                                                   Rhythm is the heartbeat element, holding
                                                                                   things together in big and little patterns.
                                                                                   Rhythm can be audible: drumbeats,
                                                                                   footsteps, finger taps, or visible: stripes,
                                                                                   dots, dashes, zigzags. Without rhythm,
                                                                                   who could be a pianist, a poet, an actor,
                                                                                   a video editor, a basketball player or a
                                                                                   juggler? Create a rap or percussion piece.
