World Children’s Festival
Produced every four years as the "Olympics" of children's imagination at The National Mall.
DESCRIPTION
A three-day celebration in our nation's capital of creativity, diversity, and unity.
OBJECTIVE
To provide a transformative experience that infuses children's creativity with empathy so they become "creative-empaths" working together for a better world.
